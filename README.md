## 🚀 How to Book a Ticket

The application interface utilizes a straightforward menu to handle your bookings. 

1. Launch the application from your desktop icon.
2. Enter your departure city and arrival city in the search fields.
3. Select your date of travel from the calendar view.
4. Click the "Search" button to see available bus routes.
5. Choose a route that fits your schedule.
6. Select your preferred seat from the seat map.
7. Click "Confirm Booking" to finalize your purchase.

The system saves your confirmation details locally for your records. You can view past bookings through the "History" tab on the main dashboard.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter difficulties, review these common solutions:

*   The application does not open: Ensure your antivirus software allows the program to run. Sometimes security settings block new installations. You may need to add the software file to your list of trusted programs.
*   Blank screen on startup: Restart your computer. This clears potential conflicts with other open applications.
*   Network error: Verify your internet connection. The application requires a stable link to retrieve updated schedules from the central database.
*   Missing text or images: Adjust your Windows display scaling settings to 100 percent. Unusual scaling sizes sometimes interfere with the layout of the user interface.
*   Update prompts: If the application asks to check for updates, press "Yes." Keeping the software version current ensures you see accurate ticket prices and availability.
